### **Product Overview**
Analytics Engine is a real-time, streaming-first analytics platform that provides on-demand KPI calculations with configurable refresh periods. The platform enables enterprises to monitor business performance across multiple value chains (Supply Chain, CRM, Sales, Financial) with real-time dashboards and comprehensive analytics capabilities.

### **Market Opportunity**
- **Target Market**: Mid to large enterprises ($50M+ annual revenue)
- **Industries**: Manufacturing, Retail, Healthcare, Financial Services
- **Problem**: Fragmented analytics, lack of real-time visibility, high costs of custom solutions
- **Solution**: Unified, real-time analytics platform with pre-built KPIs

### **Business Model**
- Annual licensing fees (value chain-based)
- Implementation services (one-time)
- Managed services (recurring)
- Infrastructure hosting (recurring)
